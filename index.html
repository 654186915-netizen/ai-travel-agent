How It Works
From Query to Itinerary in Seconds

The LangGraph agent orchestrates multiple AI reasoning steps to deliver comprehensive results.

1

Enter Your Query

Describe your ideal trip: destination, dates, hotel preferences, number of travelers.

2

AI Reasoning

The agent analyzes your request, decides which tools to call, and plans the search strategy.

3

Tool Execution

Calls Google Flights and Hotels APIs simultaneously to gather real-time availability and pricing.

4

Results Delivered

AI synthesizes all data into a clear, structured response with links, logos, and pricing.
